Chocolate Lava Cake

Indulgent individual chocolate cakes with a molten center that flows like lava when you break through the crust.

⏱
Prep Time
15 min
🔥
Cook Time
14 min
👥
Servings
4
👨‍🍳
Difficulty
Hard

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 150g unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 75g caster sugar
  • 50g plain flour
  • Butter and cocoa powder for ramekins
  • Powdered sugar for dusting
  • Vanilla ice cream for serving

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Butter 4 ramekins generously, then dust with cocoa powder. Place on a baking tray.

2

Melt chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water. Stir until smooth, then let cool slightly.

3

In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale, about 3 minutes.

4

Fold the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture gently. Sift in the flour and fold until just combined.

5

Divide batter evenly among the prepared ramekins. Bake for exactly 12-14 minutes — the edges should be firm but the center still jiggly.

6

Let rest for 1 minute, then invert onto plates. Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately with vanilla ice cream.
