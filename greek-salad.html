Classic Greek Salad

A refreshing Mediterranean salad with ripe tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, creamy feta, and a bright olive oil dressing.

⏱
Prep Time
15 min
🔥
Cook Time
0 min
👥
Servings
4
👨‍🍳
Difficulty
Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, cut into chunks
  • 1 English cucumber, halved and sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 200g feta cheese, block
  • 100g Kalamata olives
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1

Wash and prepare all vegetables. Cut tomatoes into large chunks, slice cucumber into half-moons, and thinly slice the red onion.

2

Arrange tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, and bell pepper on a large serving platter or shallow bowl.

3

Scatter Kalamata olives over the vegetables.

4

Place the block of feta cheese on top of the salad (traditionally it's served as a whole block, not crumbled).

5

Drizzle generously with extra virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar. Sprinkle with dried oregano, salt, and pepper.

6

Serve immediately with crusty bread. Do not refrigerate — this salad is best at room temperature.
