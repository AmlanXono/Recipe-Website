Butter Chicken

Rich and creamy Indian curry with tender marinated chicken in a luscious tomato-butter sauce, best served with warm naan.

⏱
Prep Time
20 min
🔥
Cook Time
35 min
👥
Servings
4
👨‍🍳
Difficulty
Medium

Ingredients

  • 800g chicken thighs, boneless
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 100g butter
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Naan bread for serving

Instructions

1

Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder for at least 30 minutes (or overnight).

2

Cook marinated chicken in a hot skillet with a little oil until charred on the outside. Set aside.

3

In the same pan, melt butter. Add cumin seeds and ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.

4

Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes until sauce thickens. Blend until smooth if desired.

5

Return chicken to the sauce. Pour in heavy cream and simmer for 10 more minutes on low heat.

6

Garnish with fresh cilantro and a swirl of cream. Serve hot with warm naan bread or steamed basmati rice.
