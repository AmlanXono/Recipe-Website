Thai Green Curry

Aromatic and creamy coconut curry with tender chicken, Thai basil, and vibrant green vegetables.

⏱
Prep Time
15 min
🔥
Cook Time
25 min
👥
Servings
4
👨‍🍳
Difficulty
Medium

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp green curry paste
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

1

Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Add green curry paste and fry for 1 minute until fragrant.

2

Add the thick cream from the top of the coconut milk. Stir and cook for 2 minutes.

3

Add chicken pieces and cook until sealed on all sides, about 3-4 minutes.

4

Pour in the remaining coconut milk, bamboo shoots, bell pepper, fish sauce, palm sugar, and lime leaves.

5

Simmer for 15 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce has thickened slightly.

6

Stir in Thai basil leaves just before serving. Serve over steamed jasmine rice.
